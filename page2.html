CELLPHONE CHARGER WITH MULTIPLE PLUG

INTRODUCTION

The Problem and Its Setting

This paper examined the helpfulness of the development of a portable cellphone charger with multiple plug, which helped the user who owned cellphones of different brands. This kind of tool was really essential especially for those who own cellular phones. Although financial chaos was still a big problem in our economy, this was not a barrier in order for someone to develop a device which accommodated four various units of cellular phones at a reasonable price.

In 1973, Dr. Marin Cooper, a former general for the systems division at Motorola, was considered the inventor of the first portable handset and the fiest pweson to make a call in a cellphone. The recharge hours of this first cellular phone was 10 hours. In the interview, National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) Chief Ronald O. Solis said he so-called penetration at 50 percent, or about 42.5 million Filipinos. This represented a growth of only about 7.5 million subscribers from the current penetration rate 40 percent. Presently, this rate has been increased by a large percentage. Thus, the use of cellular phone simply means the need of a cellular charger. Many people these days don't just havve one cellphone but most have two to three cellphones. Mobile phones chargers become a point of severe and shortfalls dynamically and openly addressed. The lifeblood of our device, phone chargers have many cases been truly demurred for wasting energy, but through this new four in one portable charger you witnessed the beginning of a revolutionary change.

This cellphone charger with multiple plug had a result of the different cellphone brands such as Nokia, Sony Ericson, Motorola and Samsung. We all know that these brands were the most common brands here in the Philippines. This cellphone charger with multiple plug portable cellphone charger were the same as the usual cellphone charger that people used that has to be plugged-in. But the unique feature in this charger were that, it was handy and smaller compared to the usual charger that was being used; if you are in a place where electricity was impossible, this portable charger can also work through a one piece triple battery: another feature in this charger were that, this cannot just generate one type of cellphone, but four type of cellular phones. The recharge time in this kind of charger was estimated to 30-45 minutes if charging. But if you were using a triple battery, it took about 45 minutes ro 1 hour in order for the battery of the cellphone to be fully charged. This charger helped users in a convenient way that when you were in a hurry to use your cellphone and less space consuming because it was small.

Statement of the Problem

This study sought to find out the cellphone charger witb multiple plug to the end-users of Calbayog City. Specifically, the researchers would like to answer the following questions stated below:

1. Is the four in one charger accepted by the target end-users?

2. How effective is the cellphone charger with multiple plug to the users or in the community?

3. What are the advantages of the said device to the end users?
