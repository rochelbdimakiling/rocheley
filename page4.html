Excercise Reflection

I learned that lists, images, audio, and video are important elements in web design. Lists help organize information in a clear way, making it easy for visitors to read and understand. Images can show what's being talked about, making the content more visual and engaging. Audio can add music or sound effects, creating a more immersive experience. Video can show moving pictures, telling a story or explaining something in a more dynamic way. When used together, these elements make a webpage more interesting and engaging, and help convey the message more effectively.
