    WHEN I WAS YOUR MAN

    "When I Was Your Man" by Bruno Mars is a soulful ballad from his album "Unorthodox Jukebox" (2012). The song is about regret and longing after a breakup, with Mars reportedly drawing inspiration from his own experiences. Featuring a piano-driven melody and heartfelt lyrics, the song showcases Mars' vocal range and emotional delivery, and it received critical acclaim and commercial success.
