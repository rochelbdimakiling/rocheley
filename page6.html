Excercise Reflection

I learned how to make forms and links using HTML. Forms are like papers that ask for information, and links are like doors that take you to other places online. I can use forms to collect information from people, and links to share more information or take people to other websites. It was cool to learn how to make these things, and I can use them to make my website more interactive and useful. Now I can create forms to get feedback or sign-ups, and add links to share more resources.
